Burger

Description

Burger is a sandwich consisting of meat & vegetables

Ingredients

  • Burger's bread
  • Burger meat
  • Oil
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Spread oil on a pan
  2. When is oil is hot, place the burger
  3. Wait until cooked
  4. Put the cheese, ketchup, tomato, pickles and the burger into the bread
