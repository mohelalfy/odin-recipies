Pizza

Description

Pizza is a famous italian dish

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olives
  • Tomato
  • Mushroom
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Spread the dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Put the tomato, mushroom & olives
  4. Cover with a layer of Mozzarella cheese
  5. Put in the oven until ready
